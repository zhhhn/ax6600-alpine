Usage

Supported Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, FTP, SFTP, BitTorrent, Metalink

Features:

  • Multi-connection downloads (up to 16 connections per file)
  • BitTorrent and Metalink support
  • JSON-RPC/XML-RPC interface
  • Lightweight and low resource usage

Example:

aria2c -x 16 -s 16 https://example.com/file.zip
aria2c --bt-seed-untrue=true torrent.torrent
